Interesting Facts About Jupiter

  • Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system, with a mass greater than all other planets combined.
  • Its Great Red Spot is a massive storm that has been active for centuries.
  • Jupiter's magnetic field is the strongest of any planet, protecting its moons from solar radiation.
  • The planet has faint rings made of dust particles, unlike Saturn's icy rings.
  • Jupiter has 92 moons, with the four largest—Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto—known as the Galilean moons.
