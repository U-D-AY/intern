Interesting Facts About venus

  • Venus is the hottest planet in the solar system due to its thick atmosphere of carbon dioxide.
  • It rotates in the opposite direction to most planets, making its days longer than its years.
  • The planet's surface is covered with volcanic plains and over 1,600 major volcanoes.
  • Venus has a dense cloud layer that reflects sunlight, making it the brightest object in the night sky after the Moon.
  • Scientists believe Venus once had water, but a runaway greenhouse effect made it uninhabitable.
