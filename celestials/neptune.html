Interesting Facts About neptune

  • Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and has the fastest winds in the solar system.
  • Its deep blue color is caused by the presence of methane in its atmosphere.
  • Neptune's largest moon, Triton, has geysers that erupt liquid nitrogen.
  • It takes Neptune 165 Earth years to complete one orbit around the Sun.
  • Neptune was discovered using mathematical predictions before it was observed directly.
